'The Moral Scoreboard'

If you can't beat'em, claim moral superiority
'Moral Scoreboard' is a simple web app that searches and returns all arrests for the time period and team specified, allowing you to effectively discount any teams achievements.
Ex.

Fan of talented team: "The Patriots are the best team in the past two decades"

Response from Moral Scoreboard User: "Aaron Hernandez was convicted of murder in 2013, your success has been sullied by the morals you bent to achieve it, the Browns have murdered exactly zero people in that time period, I prefer a team with class."
